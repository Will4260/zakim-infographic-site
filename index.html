Leonard P. Zakim Bunker Hill Bridge

Boston, Massachusetts

A Landmark of Engineering and History

Historical Facts

  • 1991–2007 — The Big Dig

    Part of the “Big Dig” project — the largest highway construction project in U.S. history.

  • Designer

    Designed by Christian Menn (Swiss civil engineer).

  • Named After

    Dedicated to Leonard P. Zakim (Boston civil rights leader).

  • 2002 — Structural Test

    14 circus elephants walked across the bridge to demonstrate strength.

  • May 17, 2003 — Opened

    Officially opened to the public.

  • Length

    Total length: 1,432 feet.

What makes it unique?
